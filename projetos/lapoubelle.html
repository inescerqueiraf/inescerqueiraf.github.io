La Poubelle

This project was created for the 2024 Taylor Design Award.
This year's theme was " The Origin of Species," and I had to create an illustration for a possible rug (spoiler alert: I didn't win).
Strongly inspired by the theory of spontaneous generation, which argued that organic matter could arise from inorganic matter, this carpet represents the first instance of human beings, garbage. If they believed that rats were "created" from a mixture of dirty clothes and wheat seeds, in other words, garbage, I can also say that human beings could easily have the same starting point. After all, we are not so far removed from the little creatures, that we think we have a sophisticated origin :-)
