Spotify Canvas

For this project, I was asked to create a Spotify canvas.
The song I chose was "Sempre Ausente", by António Variações.
Although it's contradictory, this song gives me a feeling of both insecurity and comfort. Therefore, my idea was to illustrate a small journey, cautious and melancholy, but which at a certain point, finds balance and the beauty of life.
