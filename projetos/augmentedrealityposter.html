Augmented Reality Poster

"So many tools, so little time" — was the quote that coordinated this project.
In this augmented reality interaction, after touching the poster, a pop-up window will appear. When you try to reach it, it will “run away”, followed by a second one. This will run consecutively, until the view (or work) area is saturated with notifications. Keeping up with all the tools we currently have at our disposal, is impossible. Therefore, to your displeasure, the end of this experience, will always be the same: an exhaustion loop.
Game over. :-(
